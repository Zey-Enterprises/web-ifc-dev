Coaching works best when there is a clear understanding of what it involves and what you are trying to get out of it.

There are three sensible ways to proceed from here, depending on where you are.

## Ready to apply?

If you already understand how coaching works and want to move forward, start with the intake form.

The intake is intentionally comprehensive (20–30 minutes). It replaces multiple back-and-forth conversations and allows me to assess fit with precision from the start. It is designed to help me understand not just what you want to improve, but the broader context in which those goals exist.

## What happens next

Once you submit the intake:

1. I review your goals, constraints, and current situation.

2. I assess whether coaching appears to be a strong fit.

3. If it makes sense to continue, we schedule a call or exchange details by email.

The goal is to determine fit quickly and move forward deliberately, rather than dragging things out through multiple exploratory conversations.
