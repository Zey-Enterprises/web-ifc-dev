If you are looking for thoughtful coaching rather than generic accountability, this is the right place to start.

## What happens next

1. You complete a short intake form with goals, constraints, and current challenges.
2. I review whether coaching appears to be a strong fit.
3. If it makes sense to continue, we schedule a call or exchange details by email.

## Good applications usually include

- A concrete picture of what you want to improve
- An honest account of where consistency tends to break down
- Any training, nutrition, recovery, or schedule constraints that matter
- Whether your current priority is primarily physical, emotional, intellectual, or some combination of the three
