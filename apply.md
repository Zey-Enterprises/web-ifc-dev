## What happens next

1. You complete a comprehensive intake form covering goals, constraints, and current challenges
2. I review whether coaching appears to be a strong fit
3. If it makes sense to continue, we schedule a call or exchange details by email

## Ready to apply?

If you are ready to move forward, start with the intake form.

The intake is intentionally comprehensive and usually takes about 20-30 minutes. It is designed to help me understand not just what you want to improve, but the broader context in which those goals exist.
