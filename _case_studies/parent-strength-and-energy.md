## Starting point

This client was juggling parenting, work, and inconsistent sleep. Training had become sporadic and emotionally loaded because every missed session felt like proof of failure.

## Intervention

The plan narrowed the focus to recoverable training, better weekly rhythm, and a nutrition structure that reduced reactive eating. Expectations were adjusted so the system could succeed under imperfect conditions.

## Outcome

Strength improved, session completion increased, and the client reported feeling more stable and less adversarial toward the process. This is a good example of why realistic calibration often outperforms ambition detached from context.
