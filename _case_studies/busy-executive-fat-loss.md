title: "Case Study: Fat Loss for a Busy Executive"
sort_order: 1
---

## Starting point

This client had strong intent but weak repeatability. Work travel, late meetings, and high stress made adherence inconsistent. Previous approaches relied on bursts of restriction that collapsed under normal life pressure.

## Intervention

The strategy emphasized meal structure, protein targets, a simpler restaurant decision framework, and training expectations calibrated to a volatile schedule. Recovery targets and sleep triage were treated as part of the intervention, not as optional extras.

## Outcome

Over several months, bodyweight trended down steadily, training frequency became more reliable, and the client reported lower mental friction around food. The biggest win was not only fat loss. It was replacing the feast-or-famine cycle with a workable default system.
