title: "How long should I expect to work with you?"
sort_order: 8
---

That varies. Some clients need a few months of structure and education. Others benefit from a longer cycle as goals evolve through fat-loss, maintenance, and other training phases. I regard the engagement as especially successful if I can get you to a point where you no longer need my services because you understand what to do, why to do it, and how to sustain it.
