---
title: "Can I focus on one area, or do I need the full integrated model?"
sort_order: 12
---

You can absolutely begin with one area. Some clients mainly want training or nutrition help. Others need stronger self-regulation, mindfulness, or clearer philosophical principles. The point of the integrated model is not to force equal emphasis on everything at all times. It is to recognize that the domains overlap, so progress in one area should be made in a way that supports the others.
