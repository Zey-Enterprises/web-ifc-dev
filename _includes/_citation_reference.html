Data expectations: - The citation schema is intentionally broad enough to support books, articles, reports, legal authorities, webpages, and preprints with one sane Liquid-friendly structure. Example: - Assign the source first, then pass the variable into the include: - `assign example_source = site.data.citation[source_id]` - `include _citation_reference.html source=example_source
