Travel rewards preparation. Bring or buy simple anchors: protein powder, protein bars that digest well, Greek yogurt, jerky, tuna packets, fruit, instant oats, nuts, prepared salads, rotisserie chicken, or microwaveable rice. The target is not gourmet precision; it is preventing long gaps that turn into impulsive airport or gas-station meals.

Hydration, sodium, sleep, and digestion often change during travel, so scale weight may jump. That does not automatically mean fat gain. Return to normal structure for several days before interpreting the trend.
