Use the label serving as a starting point, but weigh the powder if precision matters. Scoops are convenient, not exact measuring devices. Different powders settle differently, and a "scoop" can vary depending on how packed it is.

Track powder by grams, not by how full the shaker looks. Also remember that ready-to-drink shakes and powders can include carbohydrates and fats, so log the full product, not just the protein grams.
