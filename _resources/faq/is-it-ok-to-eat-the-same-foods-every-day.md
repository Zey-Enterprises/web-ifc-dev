Yes, within reason. Repetition is often what makes a plan work. A short list of reliable meals reduces decision fatigue and improves tracking accuracy.

The risk is nutritional narrowness or boredom. If you eat the same breakfast and lunch most days, rotate produce, protein sources, fats, and seasonings across the week. Repetition should simplify the diet, not shrink it into five foods forever.
