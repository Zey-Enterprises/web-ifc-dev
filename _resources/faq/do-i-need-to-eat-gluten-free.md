Only if you have celiac disease, wheat allergy, non-celiac gluten sensitivity, or another clear reason. Gluten-free is not automatically lower-calorie, higher-protein, or better for fat loss.

If gluten-containing foods lead to symptoms, do not self-diagnose after already removing gluten for months. Celiac testing is more reliable when gluten is still in the diet, so talk to a clinician if symptoms are significant or persistent.{% include citation.html id="lebwohl-2015-celiac-gluten-sensitivity" label="1" %}

For people without gluten-related conditions, the quality of the overall diet matters more than gluten status. Oats, potatoes, rice, beans, fruit, sourdough, whole-grain bread, and pasta can all fit depending on tolerance and goals.
