Match the database entry. Raw chicken breast and cooked chicken breast are not interchangeable entries because cooking changes water content and therefore weight. The protein and calories in the original food are mostly still there, but the scale weight changes.

For batch cooking, weigh the raw amount, cook the batch, then divide the cooked final weight into servings. If you only have cooked food in front of you, use a cooked entry and be consistent. Perfect tracking is less important than avoiding systematic mismatches.
