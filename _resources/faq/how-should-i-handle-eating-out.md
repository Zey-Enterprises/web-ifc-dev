Choose the constraint before you arrive. For fat loss, the usual wins are lean protein, vegetables, a deliberate carb or fat source, sauces on the side, and alcohol planned rather than incidental. Restaurant calories are often higher than expected because of oils, butter, sauces, portion size, and drinks.

You do not need to be perfect. You need to avoid turning uncertainty into abandon. Preview the menu, pick a meal that approximates your structure, and use conservative estimates if tracking. If eating out is frequent, build a short list of restaurants and orders that work.
