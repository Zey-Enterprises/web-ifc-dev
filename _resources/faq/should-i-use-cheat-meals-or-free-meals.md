The word "cheat" is usually unhelpful. Food is not a loyalty test. Planned flexibility can be useful, but unbounded free meals can erase a weekly deficit quickly, especially when they include alcohol, appetizers, desserts, and large portions.

Use intentional flexibility instead. Decide the meal, portions, and tradeoffs ahead of time. Keep protein in the meal, eat slowly, and return to normal structure at the next meal. If a free meal reliably becomes a free weekend, it is not serving the goal.
