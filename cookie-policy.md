This website uses cookies in a limited and straightforward way.

## What cookies are used

We use cookies for the following purposes:

### Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with the site (eg, page views, navigation patterns, and general usage trends).

These cookies help us improve the structure, content, and usability of the site. They do not identify you personally.

### Functional use

We may use basic functional cookies to support site behavior and improve user experience. For example, cookies may be used to remember preferences or maintain consistent behavior across pages.

## What we do not use

We do not use:

- advertising or remarketing cookies
- third-party tracking for behavioral advertising
- cross-site tracking mechanisms designed to profile users

## Your choices

Most web browsers allow you to control or disable cookies through their settings.

If you choose to disable cookies, some parts of the site may not function as intended.

## Changes

This policy may be updated if the way cookies are used on the site changes.
